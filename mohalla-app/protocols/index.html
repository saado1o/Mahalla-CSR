MahallaHub Protocols

Identity Verification Protocol

To maintain the sanctity and safety of the Mahalla, every new resident must undergo a mandatory document audit. This includes submitting a valid government-issued ID (CNIC) or a verified Utility Bill from the current sector. Admin approval is required before access to neighborhood features is granted.

Community Ethics & Conduct

MahallaHub is built on mutual respect. Any form of harassment, unauthorized data sharing (Purdah violation), or misuse of the Emergency SOS feature will result in immediate permanent expulsion from the digital registry.

Purdah & Privacy Mode

Residents have the right to 'Purdah Mode'. When enabled, your exact house location and profile picture are masked from general search results. Only verified community admins and emergency responders can override these settings during active SOS alerts.

Resource Sharing Guidelines

The Skill Barter and Rizq Sharing modules are strictly non-commercial. No monetary transactions are permitted within these sectors. All trades must be based on community support and volunteering.

Misuse & Penalties

False emergency alerts are treated as a breach of community trust. Multiple false alarms may lead to a temporary suspension and a review by the Mahalla Audit committee.

By entering this digital jurisdiction, you agree to uphold the dignity of the neighborly bond and the collective safety of the Mahalla.
